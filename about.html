About

My name is Luca DeJesu, and in my spare time I like making pixel art, paintings, videos (rarely), and eventually electronic music hopefully, maybe a few basic games. Pudgyfrog is an animated pixel art frog that offers you words of wisdom, accompanied by some animated pixel art scenes that I make in Aseprite (a pixel art animation tool). The actual animation format is kind of like a cutscene in a Gameboy Advance game. I realized that a while after I made the first animation, it's like a Pokemon GBA video cutscene of frog wisdom. On here, the animations don't have music currently, but on instagram I try to match some song with a similar vibe to the emotion of the pudgyfrog short.
